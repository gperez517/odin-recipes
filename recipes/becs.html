New York City Styled Bacon Egg & Cheese

The ultimate nyc breakfast staple

You can never go wrong with this on any day. Especially a day off.

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Egg
  • Cheese
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Cook the bacon
  2. Crack and whisk your eggs. Leave a lil white on 'em for that classic NYC taste.
  3. Cook eggs, add that salt & pepper. Top cheese. Cover.
  4. Toast roll/bagel. Construct sandwich, add some ketchup & mayo. Enjoy.
